Superbloom Studios was founded on the thesis that framing problems through an inclusive lens leads to better designed products and services that tap into latent markets and improve lives.

We, at Superbloom Studios, are focused on engaging with leaders to improve allyship and inclusion. For example, our recently concluded program, Superbloom Fellows, equips women professionals with the tools to own their ambition and effectively further their careers. Read more about upcoming coaching programs here.

Our strength is working at the intersection of AI and inclusivity to bridge data gaps as well as bring cutting edge AI/NLP tools to our clients. Our key project "Hidden Voices" (in collaboration with IITM RBCDSAI center) aims to reduce the gender data gap which is critical as AI tools are being built and trained on existing, but often biased data. Our Story

Superbloom Studios was founded by three best friends and college classmates from the pre-eminent Institute of Technology in India (IIT Madras). We have since gone on to be: "Hall of Fame'' awardee AI/ML and IoT expert, Dr. Raji Baskaran (Portland, USA); an astute business woman with an HBS MBA, Ms. Aruna Seshadri (Bangalore, India); and a multicultural, polyglot, software manager turned cross-cultural coach and marketing/communication expert, Ms. Kalaivani Mattern (Ludwigsburg, Germany).

The three of us share decades of lived experience and are very diverse in our fields of expertise, not to mention collaborating while living and working on three different continents. This provides us with a unique frame of reference for a successful global venture.
We are very fortunate to have many accomplished partners and advisors from a broad array of disciplines and industries. Come meet our team here!
